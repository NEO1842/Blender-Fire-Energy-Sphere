# 📜 Code of Conduct

## 🌟 Our Goal

We aim to create a respectful and creative environment for sharing Blender work.

---

## 👍 Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Give helpful feedback on visuals and techniques
- Stay focused on the project
- Support other contributors

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or abusive language
- Personal attacks
- Spam or irrelevant content

---

## 🎨 Project Context

This repository contains Blender (.blend) files.

Please keep contributions focused on:
- Visual quality
- Effects and techniques
- Improvements to the scene

---

## ⚖️ Enforcement

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation if needed.

---

## 🤝 Final Note

Let’s keep this space positive, creative, and productive.
